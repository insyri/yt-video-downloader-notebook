# YouTube Video Downloader

> *These instructions were made for users unfamiliar to Google Colab.*

This tool downloads music videos in high quality (MP4) or high-quality MP3 audio.

## How To Use

1. At the top of the screen, click the "Run all" button. Scroll all the way down until you find the UI.
2. Paste the music video URL.
3. Choose:
   - MP4 (Video)
   - MP3 (Audio only)
4. Click Download.
5. Wait for the progress bar.
6. The file will automatically download.

If you feel that you need to restart the process, go to the "Run all" button and click the small down-arrow click the "Restart session" button. Click yes when prompted. Then, in the place, click "Clear all outputs."

If you feel that the program is stalling, meaning that its taking forever to do something, you can click the "Interrupt execution" button where the other buttons, as above, are located. Then, to try again, follow the process as said above.

## Notes

- Designed for music videos (3-10 minutes recommended).
- Very long videos may be rejected.
- If an error appears, check the URL and try again.
